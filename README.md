# what the flip is skelepedia

 **skelepedia is my audodidact encyclopedia.**
   * Its the total sum of knowledge, Writings, and experiences. Ive accumulated and documentated.
   * Its my way of sharing with the world to read and learn from
   * This world is made of the basis of knowledge and you can always have more!
 
> [!IMPORTANT]
  >Some information may be wrong on skelepedia if it is please tell me!

   
# Why do this?


**Its been a life goal of mine**
 * I want the world to have more public information available to those who cant afford it or rather cant access it.
 * I would also like the world to see my perspective on life
 * Its also a way for me to leave something meaninful behind when my time comes. *im not dying anytime soon of course* 

  >[!CAUTION]
   >My views may of changed from the time of writing.
