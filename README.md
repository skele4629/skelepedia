# What the flip is Skelepedia?
**Skelepedia is my autodidact encyclopedia.**
* It's the total sum of knowledge, writings, and experiences I've accumulated and documented.
* It's my way of sharing with the world to read and learn from.
* This world is made on the basis of knowledge, and you can always have more!

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Some information may be wrong on Skelepedia. If it is, please tell me!

# Why do this?
**It's been a life goal of mine.**
* I want the world to have more public information available to those who can't afford it or can't access it.
* I would also like the world to see my perspective on life.
* It's also a way for me to leave something meaningful behind when my time comes. *I'm not dying anytime soon, of course.*

> [!CAUTION]
> My views may have changed since the time of writing.

# Important Note
* Please remember I'm **only one man** doing this, so please be gracious!
* I am not willing to open this project up to contributors. If you would like to contribute to one of my projects, please check out some others on my profile!
